Introduction

What Guinea pigs 4 rent is all about:

Here at Guinea pigs 4 rent, we believe noone should be restricted from having a piggy friend. We supply Guinea pigs to people who are unable to look after a pet full time. Our pricing models are per day, week or month. All Guinea pigs are avalaible for purchase if you decide you don't want to part with your piggy friend.

Guinea pig catalog

Below you will find the catalog of all our Guinea pigs. This will tell you their name, breed, age and if they are currently rented out or not.

Ways you can help us!

Did you know that Guinea pig's get abandened all the time! Get in touch with our rescue team to tell us about pigs in your area that need help.

You can volunteer to help out looking after our pigs. Email us to find out more: help@gp4r.co.uk
